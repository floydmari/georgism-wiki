Karl Widerquist is a political philosopher and a central scholar of the basic-income
movement. With Michael W. Howard he co-edited *Alaska's Permanent Fund Dividend:
Examining Its Suitability as a Model* (2012), the standard scholarly assessment of the
[Alaska](/wiki/alaska/) dividend as an exportable model for
[resource-rent dividends](/wiki/resource-rent-dividends-work/), and a companion volume
on exporting the Alaska model. [VERIFY: fuller biography (affiliations, other works)
