## Overview

Akhil Patel is a British economic analyst and forecaster who has built on the work of [Fred Harrison](/wiki/fred-harrison/) and [Fred Foldvary](/wiki/fred-foldvary/) to apply the **[18-year land cycle](/wiki/18-year-land-cycle/)** to global financial markets through his Property Sharemarket Economics work.

## Contribution

Patel's framework integrates land values, credit, and equity markets into a single cyclical model rooted in the Georgist analysis of land speculation. His book *The Secret Wealth Advantage* (2023) presents the cycle to a general investor audience, arguing that the recurring rhythm of land booms and busts is both predictable and rooted in the failure to tax land rent. He has been a notable popular voice forecasting the timing of the current cycle.

## Significance

Patel represents the practitioner/forecasting strand of the Georgist tradition — using the land cycle not only as social critique but as an analytical tool for understanding macro and market turning points.
