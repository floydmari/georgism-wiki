Christopher England is a historian whose work provides key historical scholarship on the single-tax and land value taxation movements. His 2023 book *Land and Liberty: Henry George and the Single Tax Movement* (Johns Hopkins University Press) is a definitive modern history of the U.S. single-tax movement. His 2018 paper in the American Journal of Economics and Sociology, *Land Value Taxation in Vancouver: Rent-Seeking and the Tax Revolt*, examines the political economy of LVT implementation and resistance in Vancouver, documenting how rent-seeking behavior shaped tax policy outcomes. Together, these works anchor historical analysis of Georgist movements in both American and Canadian contexts.
