## Overview

Scott Alexander is the pen name of an American psychiatrist who has written one of the most widely read blogs in the rationalist and effective-altruist online communities. He founded *Slate Star Codex* in 2013, which he took offline in June 2020 amid a dispute over a planned news-media profile that would have published his real name; he relaunched on Substack as *Astral Codex Ten* (ACX) in January 2021.[1]

In 2021, Alexander ran the **ACX Book Review Contest**, inviting readers to submit anonymous book reviews for judging. [Lars Doucet](/wiki/lars-doucet/)'s review of Henry George's *[Progress and Poverty](/wiki/progress-and-poverty/)* won the contest, and is widely credited as the spark of the modern Georgism revival online, reintroducing George's argument to a large, analytically demanding audience that had not previously taken land seriously as a distinct economic factor.[2] The review's success led Doucet to write the follow-up ACX series *[Does Georgism Work?](/wiki/doucet-does-georgism-work/)* and the book *[Land Is a Big Deal](/wiki/land-is-a-big-deal/)*, which Alexander is listed among the endorsers of.[3]
