## Overview

Dominic Frisby is a British author, comedian, and financial writer who has become a notable popular advocate of land value taxation in the UK, reaching general audiences that academic work does not.

## Contributions

Frisby's accessible explainers — including a widely-viewed video, *"A Simple Guide to Land Value Tax,"* and writing in outlets like *The Spectator* and his books on taxation and money — present the LVT case with clarity and humour. He frames land value taxation as a commonsense reform that taxes unearned wealth while freeing productive activity, helping carry the idea into mainstream British political debate.
