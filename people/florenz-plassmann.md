Florenz Plassmann is an economist who has co-authored multiple contributions to the land-economics literature with Nicolaus Tideman. Their 2000 paper used Markov chain Monte Carlo analysis to examine the effect of two-rate (split-rate) property taxes on construction activity. Their 2008 paper addressed the problem of providing incentives for efficient land assembly, a key challenge in urban land consolidation.
