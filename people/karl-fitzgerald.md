## Overview

Karl Fitzgerald is an Australian Georgist campaigner, researcher, and broadcaster, for many years the most visible public voice of [Prosper Australia](/wiki/prosper-australia/).

## Contributions

- **Speculative Vacancies research.** Fitzgerald led Prosper's pioneering [speculative vacancy](/wiki/speculative-vacancy/) studies, using water-consumption data to reveal how much housing sits deliberately empty — original empirical work that few others have matched.
- **Renegade Economists.** He hosted the long-running *Renegade Economists* radio show and podcast (600+ episodes), bringing land-tax, banking, and monopoly analysis to a broad Australian audience.
- **Advocacy and media.** Through Prosper and Earthsharing, he has been a tireless populariser of Georgist economics.
