## Overview

Alanna Hartzok is an American author, activist, and co-founder of Earth Rights Institute. She has been a leading voice connecting Georgist land economics with **environmental justice, peace, and human rights**.

## Contributions

Her book ***The Earth Belongs to Everyone*** (2008) collects essays arguing that capturing land and resource rent for the common good underpins a just and ecological economy — a foundational text of [ecological Georgism](/wiki/ecological-georgism/). Hartzok has worked on land-value capture and resource-rent sharing at the level of international development and UN forums, and co-founded Earth Rights Institute to advance these ideas globally. She represents the values-driven, internationalist wing of the movement.
