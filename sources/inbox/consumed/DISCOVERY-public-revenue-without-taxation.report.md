## Objections

- Mainstream economics accepts taxation as necessary; Burgess argues it is neither necessary nor just
- Demand-side school: taxation as essential instrument of fiscal and social policy
- Supply-side school: taxation justified by necessity (Nozick's argument)
- Both schools ignore the possibility of a non-tax source of public revenue
