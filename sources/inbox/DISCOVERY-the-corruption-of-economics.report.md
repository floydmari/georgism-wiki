## Concepts

1. Neo-classical Economics (NCE) · concepts · The paradigm that merged land into capital; "stratagem against Henry George" · Throughout
2. The Single Tax · concepts · Henry George's proposal to tax land rent as sole source of public revenue · Throughout
3. Land as a unique factor of production · concepts · Classical three-factor model (land, labor, capital) vs. NCE two-factor model · Throughout
4. Economic rent · concepts · Income from land not created by labor or capital; "the free lunch" vs. "transfer income" · Throughout
5. Tax capitalization · concepts · Tax on pure rent capitalized into lower land prices; neutrality of land taxation · Postscript
6. The Wedge Effect · concepts · Taxes on labor/capital create a "wedge" suppressing production; George's remedy eliminates it · Stratagem §1
7. Marginal productivity theory · concepts · George foreshadowed it; Wicksteed formalized it from George's framework · Gaffney essay Ch.3
8. Factor symmetry · concepts · Clark's doctrine that land and capital yield symmetric returns; used to deny rent is a surplus · Gaffney essay Ch.3
9. "Jelly capital" / "plastic capital" / "putty capital" · concepts · Clark's concept of capital as abstract, deathless, transmigrating essence that can ooze into land · Gaffney essay Ch.3
10. Compartmentalization · concepts · NCE defense mechanism: segregating discordant data (macro from micro, business cycles from core theory) · Gaffney essay Ch.3
11. The Henry George Theorem · concepts · Stiglitz's naming of the finding that public goods are financed optimally by land rent · Georgist Paradigm
12. Georgist paradigm · concepts · Comprehensive social philosophy: rent as public revenue, untaxing labor and capital · Throughout
13. Rent-seeking · concepts · Manipulation of politics by landowners to extract public subsidies · Main book Ch.3 (Paradigm Unearthed)
14. Land speculation and the business cycle · concepts · George's cost-push theory: cyclical overpricing of land causes depressions · Gaffney essay Ch.3
15. Uniformity doctrine · concepts · Seligman's principle that all taxes should be uniform across land and capital; basis of Tax Reform Act of 1986 · Gaffney essay Ch.3
16. The "trade-off" mentality · concepts · NCE presents choices as zero-sum trade-offs; George offered reconciliation/synthesis · Stratagem §1
17. TANSTAAFL ("There Ain't No Such Thing As A Free Lunch") · concepts · NCE slogan/shibboleth; George's insight that land rent IS a free lunch for the community · Stratagem §1
18. Physiocracy / Impôt Unique · concepts · 18th-century French school; single tax on land rent as sole public revenue · Georgist Paradigm; Gaffney essay Ch.3
