## Summary

The **Georgist Podcourse** is a structured, 12-part audio course introducing the principles of Georgist economics, produced by [Earthsharing Australia](/wiki/earthsharing-australia/) (with [Karl Fitzgerald](/wiki/karl-fitzgerald/)) and broadcast on community radio.

## Why It Matters

Unlike a conversational podcast, the Podcourse is **pedagogical** — a sequenced curriculum that builds understanding of land, rent, and taxation step by step. It is a useful free resource for someone who wants a guided introduction rather than scattered episodes, complementing the [Henry George School's](/wiki/henry-george-school/) free courses.
