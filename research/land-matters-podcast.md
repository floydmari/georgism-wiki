## Summary

*Land Matters* is the podcast of the [Lincoln Institute of Land Policy](/wiki/lincoln-institute/), covering land use, property and land taxation, housing, climate, and urban economics with researchers and practitioners.

## Why It Matters

As the audio companion to the field's premier research institution, *Land Matters* offers a more **policy- and evidence-oriented** counterpart to advocacy podcasts — useful for the empirical, institutional side of land-value questions (assessment, value capture, municipal finance). It reflects the Lincoln Institute's measured, research-first approach.
