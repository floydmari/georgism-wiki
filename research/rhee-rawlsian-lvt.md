## Summary

This 2024 paper grounds land value taxation in **John Rawls's** theory of justice rather than in efficiency or classical-liberal natural-rights arguments.

## Key Argument

Behind Rawls's **veil of ignorance** — not knowing whether one will be born to landowners or to the landless — rational agents would agree to **socialize land rent**, because land is an arbitrary, unearned endowment and capturing its rent maximally benefits the least advantaged (the difference principle). This adds a distinct moral foundation to the LVT case, alongside the efficiency argument ([zero deadweight loss](/wiki/deadweight-loss/)) and the [geolibertarian](/wiki/geolibertarianism/) natural-rights argument.
