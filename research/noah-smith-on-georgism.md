## Summary

Noah Smith, the economist and widely-read blogger (*Noahpinion*), has written several influential pieces advancing land value taxation to a mainstream economics-literate audience.

## Key Pieces

- **"A Misguided Attack on Land Value Taxes"** (2015) defends LVT against common objections, walking through the efficiency and incidence arguments.
- **"How to Sell Georgism to the Middle Class"** addresses the *political economy* of adoption — proposing framings (like social wealth funds and [citizen's dividends](/wiki/citizens-dividend/)) that could make LVT politically viable rather than merely correct.

Smith's engagement matters because he writes for a broad, influential readership and approaches LVT as a pragmatic reformer rather than a movement insider.
