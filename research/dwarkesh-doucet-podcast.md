## Summary

In this 2023 episode of the Dwarkesh Podcast, host Dwarkesh Patel interviews [Lars Doucet](/wiki/lars-doucet/) about Georgism, land value taxation, and assessment. At roughly 1h40m, it is frequently recommended as the best **single-sitting introduction** to the modern Georgist case.

## Why It Matters

The conversation covers the whole arc — why land is economically distinct, whether values are large enough to matter, the [incidence](/wiki/landlords-cannot-pass-lvt-to-tenants/) and [assessment](/wiki/land-cannot-be-assessed/) objections, and practical implementation. Patel credited it with changing how he thinks about value creation. As accessible audio, it has been a major on-ramp for newcomers.
