## Summary

In this 2018 essay, Ethereum co-founder Vitalik Buterin reviewed *Radical Markets* by Posner and Weyl and enthusiastically endorsed its central proposal — the [Harberger tax / COST](/wiki/harberger-tax/) — as an extension of Georgist principles to all property.

## Why It Matters

Buterin's endorsement was pivotal in spreading land-value and self-assessment ideas through **cryptocurrency and tech communities**, where mechanism design and digital property rights are active concerns. He explored applying Harberger-style taxes to domain names, digital assets, and other scarce resources. The essay helped make Georgist-adjacent ideas a live topic among technologists and is part of why the modern revival has strong roots in that world.
