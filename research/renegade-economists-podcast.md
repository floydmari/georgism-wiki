## Summary

*Renegade Economists* is a long-running podcast and radio program — over **600 episodes** — produced through [Prosper Australia](/wiki/prosper-australia/) and hosted for many years by [Karl Fitzgerald](/wiki/karl-fitzgerald/).

## Why It Matters

The show is one of the largest sustained bodies of Georgist audio anywhere, covering land taxation, banking, monopoly power, housing, and the [18-year land cycle](/wiki/18-year-land-cycle/) with a steady stream of expert guests. Its depth and longevity make it a significant archive for anyone wanting to follow contemporary land-economics debates from the Australian movement that has kept LVT politically alive.
