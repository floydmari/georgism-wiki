The Progress and Poverty Institute is an organization that advances land value taxation through research, scholarship and grant programs, and a researcher registry. It published *Advancing Land Value Taxation: Research Priorities for 2025 and Beyond*, which identifies key gaps in the LVT evidence base and outlines priority research settings, including New Zealand's historical pure-LVT local funding and the Gemmell et al. Auckland natural experiment. The institute maintains programs supporting LVT scholarship and serves as a substantive institutional actor in the Georgist research ecosystem.
