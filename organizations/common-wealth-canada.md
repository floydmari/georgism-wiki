## Overview

**Common Wealth Canada** is a Canadian research and advocacy organisation built on a single principle: *"natural and publicly-created wealth should be publicly shared."* It applies the Georgist analysis of [economic rent](/wiki/economic-rent/) to Canada's land, natural resources, and publicly-created value, arguing that the income from these scarce assets should fund tax relief and per-capita dividends rather than accrue privately.

## Two Main Initiatives

- **Land Value Return** — Returning publicly-created [land value](/wiki/land-value-capture/) to communities through [land value taxation](/wiki/land-value-tax/), used to lower housing costs and shift taxes off work and enterprise. Includes a focused **B.C. Land Value Tax** campaign drawing on [British Columbia's](/wiki/british-columbia/) deep historical record with site-value taxation.
- **Sovereign Wealth Fund & Citizen Dividends** — A proposed [Common Wealth Fund](/wiki/common-wealth-fund/) that would invest Canada's natural and technological [resource rents](/wiki/resource-rents/) and pay every Canadian a [citizen's dividend](/wiki/citizens-dividend/).

## Research Output

The organisation's flagship study, *[Natural Common Wealth and Economic Rent in Canada](/wiki/natural-common-wealth-economic-rent-canada/)* (2023), estimates Canada's annual economic rent at roughly **$421 billion**, of which about **$241 billion** is realistically collectable. Follow-on work models the [distributional effects](/wiki/cwc-distributional-impacts-lvt/) of an LVT-plus-income-tax-reform swap and the [price reaction](/wiki/cwc-lvt-price-reaction-model/) of land markets to LVT implementation.

## Significance

Common Wealth Canada is one of the few active organisations carrying the Georgist programme in Canada, and it is distinctive for pairing the classic land-tax argument with a modern [sovereign-wealth-fund](/wiki/common-wealth-fund/) and basic-income framing — adapting Henry George's ideas to a resource-rich federation with strong provincial control over land and minerals.
