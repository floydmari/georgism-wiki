## Overview

**Common Ground USA** is a national network of American Georgists advocating for land value taxation and the public collection of land and resource rents. It serves as a connective organisation for local activists, educators, and reformers across the United States.

## Role

Common Ground USA promotes public education on Georgist economics, supports state and local land-tax reform efforts, and links American Georgists to the international movement through the [International Union for Land Value Taxation](/wiki/international-union-lvt/). It works alongside the educational and research institutions — the [Henry George School](/wiki/henry-george-school/), the [Schalkenbach Foundation](/wiki/schalkenbach-foundation/), and the [Lincoln Institute](/wiki/lincoln-institute/) — as a grassroots advocacy arm.
