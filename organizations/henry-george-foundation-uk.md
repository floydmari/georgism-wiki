## Overview

The **Henry George Foundation** is the leading British organisation dedicated to advancing Henry George's ideas. The United Kingdom has one of the deepest Georgist traditions — George toured Britain to great acclaim, and his ideas shaped Liberal land policy and the [1909 People's Budget](/wiki/1909-peoples-budget/).

## Work

The Foundation conducts education and advocacy for [land value taxation](/wiki/land-value-tax/), publishes and hosts discussion of land economics, and connects the British movement to the international network through the [International Union for Land Value Taxation](/wiki/international-union-lvt/). It works alongside the [Labour Land Campaign](/wiki/labour-land-campaign/) and others to keep the land question present in UK policy.
