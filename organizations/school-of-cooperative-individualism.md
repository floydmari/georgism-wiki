## Overview

The **School of Cooperative Individualism** maintains one of the largest online archives of Georgist and land-economics literature: papers, essays, historical documents, and periodicals from across the [single tax](/wiki/single-tax/) movement's history.

## Role

Rather than an advocacy body, it functions as a **library and research resource** — preserving and making freely available primary and secondary sources that would otherwise be hard to find, including out-of-print works and movement periodicals. It hosts free copies of important papers (such as [Arnott & Stiglitz 1979](/wiki/arnott-stiglitz-henry-george-theorem/) and Oates & Schwab) that this wiki cites. Together with [Wealth and Want](https://www.wealthandwant.com/) and [masongaffney.org](/wiki/mason-gaffney/), it is part of the open documentary backbone of Georgist scholarship.
