From the Georgism Wiki

  • Land Value Tax — The essay's core conclusion: taxing land services is the most efficient and equitable tax.
  • Henry George Theorem — Cited as evidence that land rent suffices to fund public goods, collapsing the consumption-tax case.
  • Deadweight Loss — Explains why taxing earned income or consumption burdens production while taxing land does not.
  • Economic Rent — Defines the unearned income the essay distinguishes from labor earnings and savings.
  • Cash-Flow Tax — The expenditure-tax alternative that exempts the normal return, taxing only economic rent.
