From the Georgism Wiki

  • Ecological Georgism — Directly extends Georgist rent-capture to pollution, carbon, and resource extraction.
  • Pigouvian Taxation — Frames pollution and extraction as unpriced takings from the commons.
  • Land as Commons — The tradition that land is common property, a precondition for environmental justice.
  • Resource Rents — Treats natural-resource rent as socially capturable without efficiency loss.
  • Citizen's Dividend — Distributing collected resource rent equally as a cash dividend to all citizens.
