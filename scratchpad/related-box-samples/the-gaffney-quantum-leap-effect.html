  • EBCOR — Gaffney's theorem that excess burdens fall on rent, central to the essay.
  • ATCOR — Companion to EBCOR: taxes on labor and capital ultimately come out of rent.

  • Tideman, Akobundu, Johns & Wutthicharoen, "The Avoidable Excess Burden of Broad-Based U.S. Taxes" — Quantifies the welfare gains from shifting taxes onto land via CGE modeling.
