  • ATCOR — The theorem that untaxing labor and capital raises land rent — the essay's mechanism.
  • Tax Clawback — Harrison's argument that landowners recover taxes via untaxed land-value gains.
  • Land Speculation — The essay's boom-bust mechanism: rising values trigger speculative demand and crash.
  • Deadweight Loss — Why taxes on wages and investment suppress growth while LVT does not.
