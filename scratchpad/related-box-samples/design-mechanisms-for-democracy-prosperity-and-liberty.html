From the Georgism Wiki

  • Deadweight Loss — Deepens the essay's core claim that conventional taxes create excess burden while LVT does not.
  • Pigouvian Taxation — Extends the green tax shift mechanism with the Georgist framing of pollution as unpriced commons takings.
  • Harberger Tax (COST) — A modern mechanism-design extension of Georgist self-assessment logic beyond land to all property.
  • ATCOR — Quantifies how shifting taxes off labor and capital expands the land-rent tax base, deepening the LVT case.
  • Nicolaus Tideman — The leading theorist of both land value taxation and demand-revealing mechanisms—the essay's first two topics.
