From the Georgism Wiki

  • Henry George Theorem — Explores the theorem Stiglitz formalized: public goods funded entirely by land rent.
  • Stiglitz (2015): Land and Credit in the Distribution of Income and Wealth — Stiglitz's NBER paper arguing wealth inequality reflects rising land values, not productive capital.
  • Aggregate Land Rents, Expenditure on Public Goods, and Optimal City Size — The canonical paper co-authored by Stiglitz formalizing the Henry George Theorem.
  • Deciphering the Fall and Rise in the Net Capital Share — Empirical evidence that rising capital share is housing/land, not productive capital.
  • Narrative: The Corruption of Economics — Gaffney's argument that dropping land from neoclassical models was analytically disastrous.
