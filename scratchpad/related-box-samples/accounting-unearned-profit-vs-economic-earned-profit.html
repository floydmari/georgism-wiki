From the Georgism Wiki

  • Economic Rent — Defines the surplus the essay distinguishes from earned profit and wages.
  • Quasi-Rent — Marshall's concept directly addresses short-run returns that mimic rent but reward investment.
  • Allowance for Corporate Equity (ACE) — Modern tax design that exempts normal returns, taxing only above-normal rent.
  • Factors of Production (Land, Labour, Capital) — Classical land-labour-capital framework underpinning the essay's profit/interest distinction.
  • Cash-Flow Tax — Business tax where normal return drops out, leaving only economic rent taxed.
