From the Georgism Wiki

  • Wages Fund Doctrine — Classical wage theory George refuted to explain poverty amid progress.
  • Margin of Production — Explains how wages are set by land access, not legislation.
  • Deadweight Loss — Explains why price floors like minimum wages cause welfare losses.
  • Monopsony — Buy-side market power lets employers suppress wages below competitive levels.
  • Rent-Seeking — Using political power to capture wealth rather than create value.
